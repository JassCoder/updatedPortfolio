Case Study

Luxe Dining Landing

Build a premium landing page that feels expensive, loads fast, and reads perfectly on mobile.

Overview

The client needed a high-end restaurant landing page with clear CTA flow and strong visual hierarchy. Constraint: keep it light and fast, no dependencies.

Problems

  • Generic typography made the brand feel cheap.
  • Sections weren’t scannable on mobile.
  • Hover states and motion were either missing or too loud.

Solutions

  • Fluid typography using clamp() and controlled line-length.
  • Card-based layout with consistent spacing system.
  • Subtle motion: hover elevation, fades, gradient underline accents.

Outcome

  • Premium feel with minimal visual noise.
  • Mobile-first layout that stays readable.
  • Structure that scales to a full multi-page site.
